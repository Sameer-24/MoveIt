The Process of House Shifting

If you hire a full service of one of the movers and packers for the house shifting through this portal, then you will get complete peace of mind with full assistance. You will have to do nothing but relax and see how well your move is executed. You will get assistance from start to finish. 

Tasks done by movers at your old residence

  • Create an inventory of entire household effects
  • Pack each article of household effects properly
  • Pack fragile items with special care
  • Label each box with the appropriate tag
  • Dismantle furniture for easy wrapping & loading
  • Wrap furniture pieces in moving blankets
  • Make appliances & pack them to move
  • Load entire household effects onto the truck

Tasks done by movers at your new residence

  • Unload the truck with care to avoid damage to goods
  • Place boxes, furniture & appliances into your new home
  • Unpack boxes and unwrap items with care
  • Reassemble and setup furniture (optional)
  • Reassemble utilities and appliances (optional)
  • Re-arrange items as per your instructions (on demand)
  • Clean junk such as torn packing supplies (optional)
  • Freebies – peace of mind
